## 7. Data & Infrastructure Policy

### Data Retention
All hosted plans (Options 1 & 2) operate on a **12-month rolling retention policy**. Data older than 12 months is automatically archived. Extended retention of up to 6 additional months is available as a paid add-on at **$10/month**.

### Storage & Logging
All tiers operate at **1-minute logging intervals**, consuming approximately **50MB per device per year**. Storage allocations per tier are designed with a 2x buffer above actual consumption to ensure smooth operation.

### Infrastructure for Ultra & Industrial Tiers
Clients on the **Ultra** (500 devices) or **Industrial** (1,000+ devices) tiers may require a dedicated database arrangement separate from our shared hosting environment. This will be communicated transparently and factored into your pricing quote.

### Payments
Monthly subscriptions are billed in USD. Local payment via **MTN Mobile Money** and **Airtel Money** is supported for Ugandan clients. UGX billing can be arranged on request.
